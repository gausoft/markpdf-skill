# Linear for Schools

Project management built for educators

---

## The problem

Teachers spend **11 hours/week** on admin work outside of teaching.

- Lesson planning scattered across Google Docs, Notion, paper
- No shared visibility across grade-level teams
- Existing tools (ClassDojo, Google Classroom) are student-facing — not teacher-facing

---

<!-- _class: stats -->

# 3.7M
US teachers underserved by current tooling

---

## Our solution

A Linear-style workspace built for the rhythms of a school year.

- Lesson plans as **issues**
- Units as **projects**
- Curriculum standards as **labels**
- Built-in templates for the 12 most common subjects
